Welcome and behold the Museum of Alternative Facts’ wondrous new exhibit:
The Amazing and Totally Legitimate Discovery of Ancient Lands.
Brace yourselves, for what you witness here on this exhibit will shock you. The existence of lands and places that were previously only considered myths are now proven real and not fabricated at all.
We can thank these unbelievable discoveries to the brilliant Chris Topher-Columbus; a long-time frequent visitor to the Museum of Alternative Facts and whom, as we have been informed by the great man himself, has recently returned safe from his travels to his studio apartment in Frankston.
We pay tribute to his endeavours and ask you to sit back and enjoy this spectacular exhibit.

Shangri La

First mentioned in James Hilton’s 1933 novel Lost Horizon, the wonderful oriental land of Shangri La has, until now, never been been visited by a man of the outside world.
The location of this Himalayan wonder, buried deep within the valleys of the great Kunlun Mountains, was revealed to Chris via the whisperings of his Vietnamese Uber driver during a 3am trip to Frankston South McDonald’s. Not a word more was needed for Chris to pack his bags and become the first modern-world man to see this exotic utopia for himself.
From the descriptions disclosed to us by Chris, Shangri La is "your typical jungle looking forest - like some real Tarzan Disney stuff" except "there’s Chinese people there that are old as because the water there has special powers"; simply an astonishing outlook into such an earthly paradise.

El Dorado

A city of gold and riches - sounds like some sort of fantasy right? Well not anymore! The Museum of Alternative Facts can reveal that the legend of El Dorado is in fact a true and very real location in the heart of Colombia.
For those not familiar with the legend; the golden city of El Dorado was thought by 16th century Spain as the source of all gold in South America due to the fact that gold was so frequently found in possession of the natives living along the northern coast. Countless expeditions were failures and numerous lives were lost in the quest to find such fruitful land to no prevail.
At this present time, however, we can reveal that this eye-opening legend is a mystery no more! Our amazing pioneer Chris Topher-Columbus has ventured into the unknown forests surrounding Lake Guatavita (the lake said to be a worshipping venue of El Dorado) to capture these stunning and genuine images.
How did Chris know where to look you say? "I was sitting in Taco Bill one day and, being the cultured man I am, tried to order some Doritos in the waiter's native tongue. I said ‘El Dorito?’, and from my solid knowledge of the Spanish language, I was able to decipher the hispanic man’s firm response into the secret location of the city of gold".

Atlantis

We come now to perhaps our most spectacular section of the exhibit that the Museum of Alternative Facts is proud to present to you today. This particular land has been researched for centuries in hopes of its eventual discovery by the most accomplished historians and experienced explorers without success.
Ever since its mention of existence by the ancient Greek works of Plato, no one had ever known exactly where in the Atlantic Ocean the lost civilisation of Atlantis had sunk after falling out of favour with the gods (who of course still are an active force in life today); absolutely no one, until the fantastic Chris Topher-Columbus.
For his final venture to find the lost city, Chris developed a custom-built submarine by transforming his Nan’s old fishing boat into a high tech deep-sea submersible, being the genius that he is. With a secret charter showing the exact depth and position of the city (handed to him via a pdf in response to his question posted on Yahoo Answers ‘Where Atlantis at?’), finding the lost city was a simple feat.
Though what is truly amazing about the lost city is not merely its existence, but what inhabitants dwell within its beauty. Chris enlightens us of an entirely new species that he has taken upon himself to name “Fish-People”. According to our great pioneer, Fish-People resemble the characteristics of what some may refer to as ‘mermaids’ and live just like us here on the surface "except they have gills and can speak underwater and stuff!"
